1. “Offerings are unique. In other words, multiple offerings on the same day and time slot must be offered at a different location.

context Offering
inv requirement1: 
    OfferingCatalog.allInstances()->forAll(o1, o2 | 
        o1 <> o2 implies 
        (o1.date <> o2.date or o1.startTime <> o2.startTime or o1.location <> o2.location))



2. “Any client who is underage must necessarily be accompanied by an adult who acts as their guardian.”

context Client
inv requirement2: 
    self.isUnderage implies self.guardianId <> null



3. “The city associated with an offering must be one the city’s that the instructor has indicated in their availabilities.”

context Offering
inv requirement3: 
    self.instructor.availabilities.cities->includes(self.location.city)



4. “A client does not have multiple bookings on the same day and time slot.” 

context Booking
inv requirement4:
    Booking.getClientBookings(self.clientId)->forAll(b | 
        b = self or b.date <> self.date or b.startTime <> self.startTime)
